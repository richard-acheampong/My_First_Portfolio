About Me

I’m Richard Acheampong—a ministry leader, creative systems builder, and media strategist based in Kpone, Ghana.

My work sits at the intersection of faith, design, and digital innovation.

I thrive in environments where story meets structure, and where clarity isn’t just a goal—it’s a discipline.

Over the years, I’ve led worship movements, coordinated creative residencies, managed media platforms, and facilitated teaching ministries.

My contributions span spiritual formation, digital storytelling, and scalable leadership systems.

Whether guiding a team, designing a user interface, or delivering a message, I focus on creating experiences that move hearts and sharpen minds.

>My approach is rooted in African visual heritage, systems thinking, and a commitment to sustainable impact.

I’m currently deepening my front-end development skills through the ALX program and refining my public speaking through Toastmasters.

These disciplines complement my work in building intuitive platforms, leading creative teams, and mentoring others. I believe in designing systems that outlive me and stories that outlast trends—whether through code, worship, or community engagement.
